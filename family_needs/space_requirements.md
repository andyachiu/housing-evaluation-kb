# Space Requirements

## Minimum Specs
- **Bedrooms:** 2 (Highly prefer 3 bedrooms)
- **Bathrooms:** 2
- **Min Square Footage:** ~950+ sqft (looking for additional space compared to current)

## Must-Haves
- Attached garage with ample storage space.
- Backyard/garden with proximity to nature/parks.
- Quiet, safe, low-traffic street.

## Nice-to-Haves
- Third bedroom with its own separate access (ideal for guests or home office).
- Modern, spacious kitchen (avoid dated and small kitchens).
- Modern, spacious bathrooms (avoid dated and small bathrooms).
- Minimal stairs / single-level living (avoid properties with a lot of stairs).

## Dealbreakers
- Dated and small kitchen or bathrooms.
- Excessive stairs.
- Complete lack of backyard or proximity to green space/parks.

## Notes
Tours of detached homes (e.g., JC House) confirmed that additional space, storage, and private yard/nature access are major draws, but dated finishes and high stair counts are significant negatives.
